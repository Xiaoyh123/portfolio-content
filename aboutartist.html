Hi everyone, my name is Vicky, a Bay Area Native who grew up in neighborhoods with bug art scenes. In the diverse enviroment, I was able to explore different passions and found the love of art. I am a lover of creating and building things digitally and physically. I've been practicing pottery since I was 15 years old and now teach students fromthe comfort of their home. Please support me and my art!
