Vicky Huang

 

Hi everyone, my name is Vicky, a Bay Area Native who grew up in neighborhoods with big art scenes. In the diverse enviroment, I was able to explore different passions and found the love of art. I am a lover of creating and building things digitally and physically. Ceramics has played a huge role in my life and I'm grateful I can share my pieces with you all. The process never ceases to amaze me and I hope it never does!I've been practicing pottery since I was 15 years old and now teach students from the comfort of my home. Please support me and my art!
